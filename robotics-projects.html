Gismo - My Multi-Sensor Bot

Overview

Gismo is my most ambitious project to date! It's a multi-sensor robot packed with a variety of functions:

  • **Chatbot:** Gismo can engage in basic conversations, responding to simple questions and prompts.
  • **Game:** Gismo can play simple games like tic-tac-toe, challenging users to a match.
  • **Tamagotchi:** Gismo has virtual needs, requiring users to "feed" and "play with" it to keep it happy.
  • **Self-balancing:** Gismo can maintain its balance using gyroscopic sensors, even when nudged or placed on an uneven surface.
  • **Ballbot:** Gismo can also navigate using a ball for movement, adding to its maneuverability.

Challenges and Lessons Learned

Creating Gismo was a challenging but rewarding journey. Some of the hurdles I encountered included:

  • **Calibration:** Fine-tuning the sensors for accurate readings and stable self-balancing.
  • **Integration:** Combining the different functionalities (chatbot, game, Tamagotchi) into a cohesive system.
  • **Power Management:** Ensuring efficient power usage for all the components and sensors.

Through these challenges, I gained valuable experience in problem-solving, code optimization, and electronics debugging. I also deepened my understanding of robotics kinematics and control systems.

Future Development

I have exciting plans for Gismo's future development, including:

  • **Enhanced AI:** Improving the chatbot's conversational abilities and potentially integrating a voice recognition system.
  • **Computer Vision:** Adding a camera to enable Gismo to "see" and interact with its environment.
  • **Remote Control:** Implementing remote control functionality for more interactive play and exploration.
