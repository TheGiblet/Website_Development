Target Earth Robot: A Replica of the 1954 Alien Invader

Do you love sci-fi movies and collectables? You will love this 3D model of the robot from Target Earth, a 1954 independently made American black-and-white science fiction film that follows a small group of people who survive a sudden invasion by hostile robotic beings believed to be from the planet Venu. This 3d replica is based on the original movie design, a ridged body, and of Venusian origin.
